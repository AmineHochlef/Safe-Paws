International Cat Day: When is it and how to celebrate

International Cat Day frequently asked questions

What is International Cat Day?

International Cat Day is a special day held every year to honor cats. People can celebrate the holiday by promoting awareness about cats and how we can help them thrive.

When is International Cat Day?

International Cat Day is held each year on August 8. You can also celebrate National Hug Your Cat Day on June 4 or National Dress Up Your Pet Day on January 14.

How can pet owners celebrate International Cat Day?

There is never a shortage of ideas when looking for ways to celebrate International Cat Day. Some of the ways you and your audience can honor the holiday include:

  • Volunteering at your local shelter
  • Going to a cat cafe
  • Donating to a cat charity
  • Adopt a cat
  • Watching “The Aristocats”
Share Educational Information

One of the primary goals of International Cat Day is to spread awareness about the health and safety of cats. The holiday is a great chance to invite your followers to share their advice about owning cats. For example, the People’s Dispensary for Sick Animals (PDSA) created an online quiz about feline flu and posted it on International Cat Day. Pet owners could take the quiz, learning a lot about the illness. Another idea is to invite your audience to share experiences, advice, pet care tips, and stories. You can create a post and ask your followers to post their responses in the comments. You could also create a unique link and post responses you receive in individual posts. Offer a giveaway prize as an incentive for people to participate. Select the winner of your prize at random from the entries.

Offer rewards and coupons

International Cat Day is a great chance to engage your audience, but you could also use this as an opportunity to reward them. You can use creative promotions to drive more traffic to your website, increasing your sales. For example, you could make a social media post encouraging your followers to sign up for your brand’s newsletter. Once they sign up, they’ll get entered into a giveaway for free cat products. You could also offer your subscribers and followers coupons to redeem on certain products.
